Our Story

Angie Spa & Cuts was founded by Angie in 2020 with a vision to create a sanctuary where clients could experience both relaxation and style under one roof. Our mission is to provide premium spa and grooming services that help our clients relax, refresh, and redefine their look.

In 2023, we expanded our services to include a dedicated barber station, creating a unique dual experience that caters to both spa enthusiasts and those seeking precision cuts and grooming.

Our team of certified professionals is dedicated to providing exceptional service in a welcoming and luxurious environment. We continuously train in the latest techniques and use only premium products to ensure the best results for our clients.
